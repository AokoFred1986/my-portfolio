About Me

My academic journey has been rich and diverse, rooted in a passion for international relations, political science, law, and technology, all converging in the realm of energy and environmental law. My academic foundation spans continents and disciplines, reflecting my commitment to a holistic understanding of global issues. With a Ph.D. in International Relations and Political Science from Monarch Business School of Switzerland, complemented by a range of degrees including BSc in Information Science, LLB Hons, and LLM in Energy & Environment from the University of Birmingham, I have honed my analytical skills and developed a nuanced perspective on legal and policy challenges. My professional credentials further attest to my dedication to excellence and expertise in dispute resolution and international law. As a CIArb-certified arbitrator in Kenya and London, and a member of esteemed bodies like the London Court of International Arbitration Africa User Council, I have navigated complex legal landscapes with acumen and integrity. Additionally, my certifications in FIDIC adjudication and experience in family mediation demonstrate a versatile skill set crucial for addressing multifaceted issues in energy and environmental law. My academic and professional journey has been augmented by practical experiences and a commitment to innovation. I have engaged with cutting-edge legal frameworks through programs at Harvard Law School and M.I.T., exploring the intersection of law, AI, and data science. These experiences have equipped me with a forward-thinking mindset and the tools to navigate the evolving complexities of modern legal challenges.
